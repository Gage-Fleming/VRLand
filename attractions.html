Explore 50+ Unique Worlds

With over 50+ unique attractions, we could not forgive ourselves for showing you all we have to offer online. However, we know the below will get you to the doors for a fabulous family stay!

Explore Hyrule

Hyrule is a world with extensive lore and experiences to be had. You’ve always viewed this world through a screen. Now you can experience the world through VRLand! Fight alongside Link against Ganon and rescue the princess Zelda in our new attraction and theme-area.

You're sure to be hankering for a meal after you save a kingdom. Don't go hungry, be sure to stop in at the Dueling Peaks Stable to revitalize yourself with some Zelda staples! You spent time collecting the food in the ride. Now reap your rewards! Some of the staples include:

  • Hearty Fried Wild Greens
    • A basic vegetable dish made by sauteing fresh wild plants
  • Enduring Elixir
    • Restore your stamina and receive a single-use fast-pass ticket back to Hyrule
  • Mighty Simmered Fruit
    • This sweet dish is made by heaping tasty fruits into a pan and simmering until tender

Explore our Worlds History

Earth has been something you have experience all your life. However, the stories it has seen and the experiences that have been shared are locked away in our history textbooks. Now you can experience some of the worlds defining moments at VRLand. Our Colossal-Reality coaster brings you on a fast paced adventure through time!

While you navigate this portion of our park, many of our worlds greatest historical figures will guide you along your journey. Lincoln, Ghandi, and many more await your arrival! Be sure to say hello and the mixed reality ai will be able to converse just as our understanding of these figures would.

Seasons Greetings

Throughout the year a portion of our park will be cut off to explore seasons traditions. Enjoy a ride with Santa, or an easter egg extravaganza with the Easter Bunny! All four seasons will get a fantastic unique world to explore!

Throughout Spring, you can enjoy Easter, Sham el Nessim, Songkran and Holi in unique VRLand experiences.

During Summer you can experience, midsummer, Aomori Nebuta Matsuri and Puck Fair. All of these will be outdoor VR experiences!

Enjoy the fall season with hand-crafted Halloween, Bonfire Night, Chuseok, and Dia De Los Muertos VRLand experiences.

Finally, when you want to stay warm during winter you can experience some wonderful VRLand experiences pertaining to: Christmas, Hanukkah Kwanzaa and Three Kings Day.
